📊 Customer Segmentation & Targeting Strategy Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on identifying different customer segments based on purchasing behavior and profitability using Power BI.

The objective is to help businesses understand:

Who their most valuable customers are

How customers contribute to revenue and profit

What strategies can be applied to increase customer lifetime value

🎯 Objectives

Segment customers based on total sales contribution

Analyze customer profitability

Identify high-value customers

Provide targeted marketing recommendations

🛠 Tools Used

Power BI Desktop

Superstore Sales Dataset

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

📂 Dataset Details

The dataset includes:

Customer Name

Sales

Profit

Quantity

Region

Category

Order Date

Data was cleaned and transformed using Power Query Editor.

📊 Key Metrics Created

Customer Sales

Customer Profit

Customer Segment Type (High / Medium / Low Value)

Segmentation was based on total sales contribution.

📈 Dashboard Features

Customer Segment Distribution (Pie Chart)

Segment vs Total Sales (Bar Chart)

Segment vs Profit (Bar Chart)

Regional Distribution of High-Value Customers

Interactive Filters

🧠 Customer Segmentation Logic

Customers were classified as:

High Value Customers → Sales above threshold

Medium Value Customers → Moderate spending

Low Value Customers → Low spending

This helps in prioritizing marketing efforts.

💡 Business Insights

High-value customers contribute significantly to total revenue.

A small percentage of customers generate majority of profit.

Certain regions have higher concentration of premium customers.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Offer loyalty programs for high-value customers.

Target medium-value customers with personalized promotions.

Run awareness campaigns to increase low-value customer engagement.

📈 Business Impact

This segmentation model helps management:

Improve marketing ROI

Increase customer retention

Optimize revenue strategy

Focus on high-impact customer groups
